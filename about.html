Who is Cristiano Magrini

I am a teenager from the province of Bergamo and study IT at ITIS P. Paleocapa. Since childhood, I have been passionate about math and technology, anxiously waiting for the rare IT lessons every month. After receiving a scholarship for my last year of middle school, I chose to continue my studies in the IT field. I think I made the right decision because I am passionate about what I learn in school, and my favorite fields are programming, cybersecurity, home automation and network management.

Since the first year of middle school, I have annually participated in math and logic games organized by the school. In 2022 I reached the national finals of the Giochi Matematici del Mediterraneo, finishing 11th in my age category. For the past two years, I have also participated in the Computer Science and Cybersecurity Olympiad. In 2023, after passing the territorial selections, I had the opportunity to improve my skills in solving network, web, and software security challenges during a Training Camp at the ITC ILO Campus in Turin, thanks to OliCyber. In 2024 I did a two-week school internship as a programmer at the company SORINT.lab. During this time I worked with developers to solve tasks, took training courses, and created this website. It was a meaningful experience because it allowed me to apply the knowledge I gained in school and understand how a professional work environment operates.

I am a big fan of football, which I have played for 11 years. I love going to the stadium to cheer for my team and I follow statistics and international transfer market with interest. I'm also a console gamer, who prefers sports games like FIFA and NBA2K, as well as action-adventure games like Assassin's Creed and Marvel's Spider-Man. Besides sports and video game interests, I love to go to the cinema with my friends and follow TV series on streaming platforms, which are a real addiction for me. Musically, I mainly listen to hip-hop, rap and trap, and going to concerts of my favorite artists is one of the experiences that excites me the most.
